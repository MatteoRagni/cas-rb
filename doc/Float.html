    class Float

    Overloading operators for Float. Operations that are oveloaded are:

    • `overloaded_plus` for `+`

    • `overloaded_minus` for `-`

    • `overloaded_mul` for `*`

    • `overloaded_div` for `/`

    • `overloaded_pow` for `**`
